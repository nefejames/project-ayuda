about us

ProjectAyuda is a Non-Profit Organization connecting NGOs to funding through partnerships with event managements, campaigns and sales, providing support through skilled and willing volunteers. ProjectAyuda is partnering with NGOs to improve the standard of living of Nigerians, Africans and the global community, in line with ONE Campaign’s vision to end extreme poverty and preventable diseases by 2030 and the United Nations 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development that includes 17 Sustainable Development Goals with poverty being a top priority. Building on the principle of “leaving no one behind,” the new Agenda emphasizes a holistic approach to achieving sustainable development for all.

our mission

“Ayuda” is help in Spanish, this is reflective of the project’s aim to partner with NGOs to touch as many lives as possible, giving hope and providing a safe haven for vulnerable women and children, and people living with disabilities. Project Ayuda seeks to connect NGOs with the funding and support required to effect change in communities in need.

learn more

our vision

We aim to improve the standard of living of people across the global community with Nigeria as the immediate focus. As a global community, it is our collective responsibility to help each other through the rigors of life, a little kindness at the right time could be a game changer. In line with this vision, we are partnering with NGOs in achieving a poverty free community where human lives are valued and protected.
